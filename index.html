    The world of edc

    In this place, we affirm that we are kindred spirits bound by deep passion — not only for the music we love, but for the community that surrounds it. We groove to the same universal beat and, together, create beautiful experiences that will never be forgotten. With that spirit in mind, Insomniac has taken EDC to new heights for our next journey together.

    Coming to Vegas for the first time ever, EDC Camping is a dream come true for many, extending the festivities into the daytime for a complete four-day experience. Our earlier hours will also allow us to create an opening ceremony on the cosmicMEADOW lawn from 4–7pm, featuring extended sets from a different artist each day! Plus, earlier festival dates mean cooler springtime weather for all to enjoy.

    We can’t wait to share all of these new elements and celebrate with you Under the Electric Sky!

    Stages

    kineticFIELD

    Always in motion, forever evolving, this sacred edc-wrapper has known homes across the globe, continually transforming itself as much as it has those who experience it. What grand new form will it take this year? Search your heart, for in love lies the answer.

    cosmicMEADOW

    Long known for its lush greenery, stellar views, and bold musical experimentation, this majestic meadow will be transformed into a holy site for enchanted ritual, performed each day in the opening ceremonies of EDC.

    circuitGROUNDS

    As much as we worship the night sky, we are rooted to the land on which we dance. We are connected, one to another, to the past and to the future, and to the energy all around us. Look to circuitGROUNDS to spark your passion for epic melodies.

    quantumVALLEY

    A new world is about to unfurl — one where the sky has no limits. Welcome to our utopia, where ethereal beats and timeless trance melodies transport you to a higher state of consciousness. This is your destination.

    bassPOD

    Something massive this way comes. A primal frequency rumbles beneath the foundation of EDC, a harbinger of unforeseen forces that approach. Prepare to be shaken by the purest forms of dubstep, trap, and drum & bass.
